Calorie Counting is tough
Simplify you life by using talorie.
Keeping track of calories is proven to be one of the most effective ways of managing your diet.
Why over complicate it?
Talorie simplies this by estimating daily calories using tallys. By using "ball-park" estimates, you can spend less time tracking your diet, and more time on the things that really matter.
Tallys:
  • Nutrition Tally: 1 = 50 Calories
  • Water Tally: 1 = 8oz of Water
  • Workout Tally: 1 = 50 Calories Burned
